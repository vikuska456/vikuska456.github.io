APPLY NOW

Applications for 2018 are officially open, the countdown to the Summer of a lifetime starts right here!

Get ahead of the game and apply early, the quicker you apply the quicker you will be placed! Sign up below to start your application, you’ll be on that plane before you know it.

Note: you must have an approved IEC to apply for the support staff role. Applying can be done in three steps:

Complete the short application below and make the stage one payment of £69. You’ll then be sent a more in depth application from the Camp Canada Team to complete. Once you’ve sent your complete application over (to info@campcanada.org), your application will be screened and you’ll be assigned for an interview with one of our fantastic Campus Managers. You’ll then complete 3 references, a second stage payment and a short video to show off how great you are. We'll work with camps across Canada to place you! Simple!
